Meet your instructor

Hello, from Anjali!


I am a native Indian and I was born and raised in India. I have been working on my content for more than 3 years, with followers from all over the world. I had the pleasure of preparing more than 500 recipes, all vegan!

Initially, I was exclusively filming videos for fun, but I soon realized that I wanted to help more people in learning the cuisine. That’s why I started offering online courses!

I believe that my online cooking course is very effective because you can follow the lessons when it’s most convenient for you and you can review the concepts as many times as you want. In other words, you learn at your own pace with no pressure and without the need to attend a class.

And what's even better is that you are not alone because you will get access to groups of like-minded students who want to cook as much as you do. Also, you can text me and ask me questions anytime, I love answering them! Let's go and cook together!
